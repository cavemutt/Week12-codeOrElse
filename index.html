Our Proven Methods

A quick glance at the award-winning services that you'll receive.

Our method is clinically proven to motivate anyone to learn coding at lightning speed
...or else you might end up in a clinic.

Our staff will meet with you and your family to hammer out clear objectives and timelines. Deadlines will be in print, on calendars, in your calendar apps, and scratched very finely into your eyeballs.

In addition to having a staff member onsite in your home, we will also gain access to all of your email addresses, social media accounts, and phone apps to make sure you are always on target for the next goal deadline.

Per our 100-page contract, only we reserve the ability to cancel your membership payments, and only once you have completed all objectives. If you are falling behind, we will automatically bump you up to the next payment tier. The longer you take to learn to code, the more money we take out of your accounts(which we will also gain full access to).

We have several tools in our arsenal to get you back on track if you fall behind. Our patented Secret Agent Cats will be watching you and your family's every move, all day, everyday, and making sure YOU know it while earning your family's trust and affection. If you miss a deadline, we be around every corner, behind every bush, calling you, your family, and your boss incessantly, not letting you sleep and interrupting every attempt to eat, until you have caught up to your coding objectives.

We feel that when people think they want to give up, that they really just need a "push" in the right direction to make some changes...if you know what I mean.
